Design of Naturalistic VR using Oculus Rift

Author and filmmaker David Marlett is researching and developing what he calls "cinemersia," the cinematic, storytelling, and artistic techniques necessary to produce feature length, live-action, narrative 'films' to be experienced within head-mounted displays ("HMDs") such as the Oculus Rift or Sony Morpheus. His narrative/story work is in concert with the numerous innovators currently developing advanced HMDs, 3D/360 cameras, and workflow/distribution technologies.
